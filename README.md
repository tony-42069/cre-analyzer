Does My Deal Pencil? ✏️
Does My Deal Pencil? is a web-based tool designed to help real estate investors analyze the profitability of commercial real estate deals. With this calculator, you can quickly evaluate important financial metrics such as Internal Rate of Return (IRR), Net Present Value (NPV), Cash-on-Cash return, and Cap Rate, helping you determine whether a deal pencils out.

Features
Real-Time Deal Analysis: Input your deal details, and instantly receive an analysis of key investment metrics.
Interactive Form Fields: Add your deal-specific parameters like Purchase Price, NOI Growth Rate, Loan-to-Value, and more.
Detailed Results: The tool provides IRR, NPV (10% discount rate), Cash-on-Cash Return, and equity multiple to evaluate your investment.
Deal Indicator: A visual indicator (✅, ⚠️, ❌) helps you easily understand the investment potential.
How It Works
Purchase Price: Enter the purchase price of the property.
Annual NOI (Net Operating Income): Provide the current annual NOI.
NOI Growth Rate: Estimate the annual percentage increase in NOI.
Holding Period: Define how long you plan to hold the property.
Exit Cap Rate: Estimate the cap rate at which you plan to exit.
Loan-to-Value (LTV): Input the percentage of the purchase price covered by a loan.
Interest Rate & Amortization: Set the interest rate and amortization period of the loan.
CapEx & Closing Costs: Include annual capital expenditures and closing cost assumptions.
